The Opportunity

Why Da-Zero is perfectly positioned

"Da-Zero" means "from scratch." That's not just a name - it's a content strategy waiting to happen. Here's what we see.

  • 01

    The "from scratch" story is gold

    Every dish made from scratch is a piece of content. Dough being stretched, sauces being built, pasta being cut. This is exactly the type of content that goes viral on TikTok and Instagram. ASMR food content, process videos, behind-the-scenes. Da-Zero has this built into its DNA.

  • 02

    No one in Chester-le-Street is doing this

    We looked at every restaurant in the area. None of them are producing consistent, high-quality short-form video. The first one to do it properly owns the local market. That should be Da-Zero.

  • 03

    You already have the love

    Your Google reviews are strong. Customers already call you a "hidden gem." The food speaks for itself. What's missing is the distribution. We put the food in front of thousands of new people every week.

  • 04

    Consolidate and dominate

    Right now you have multiple websites and Instagram accounts splitting your presence. We consolidate everything into one powerful brand, one website, one account, one strategy. Every view, every follower, every booking flows into one place.

  • 05

    TikTok is untouched

    There's already a customer review video on TikTok rating Da-Zero 10/10. But it's on someone else's account. You need your own. Restaurants posting consistently on TikTok are seeing direct increases in bookings. Some go viral overnight.
