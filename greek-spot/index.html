The Spot

Modern vibes,
Mediterranean soul.

The Greek Spot is a small family bakery and cafe in the middle of Middlesbrough. The recipes are the ones we grew up with in Greece: phyllo stretched by hand in the air, syrup poured while the baklava is still warm, coffee shaken the way it is on every Greek street corner.

Everything in the counter is baked that morning. When a tray goes, it goes. Come early, stay a while, and let us recommend something.

Family run
