The Direction We Discussed
The Plan, in Plain English

✓ Taper Yellow Pages down slowly, do not go cold turkey

Right now most of your budget sits with Yellow Pages. We do not switch that off overnight. We ease it down gradually so there is never a gap in enquiries, and you keep the listing live so the reviews and reputation you have built up stay exactly where they are.

✓ Shift the momentum across to Facebook and social

As Yellow Pages tapers, we build your presence on Facebook and beyond with a steady stream of content, your real jobs, before and afters and the satisfying work. That builds an audience that is yours, that you do not pay a directory to rent.

✓ Point all the traffic at your website and enquiry form

You already send people to your own enquiry form, which is exactly right. We make your website the hub everything flows to, so every enquiry comes straight to you rather than through a third party, and you own the relationship from the first click.

✓ Website ready first, then turn the ads on

When we do add paid ads, we get the website set up and optimised first, so there is somewhere proper for that traffic to land and convert. There is no point paying to send people to a page that is not ready. Website first, ads second.

How We'd Roll It Out
A Sensible Order
  1. Start the content. We begin filming around your jobs and posting consistently, building your audience and a library of before-and-afters from day one.
  2. Set up the website. We optimise the site and automate your enquiry form, so there is a proper home for all the traffic we are about to send.
