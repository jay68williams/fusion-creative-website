The Direction We Discussed
The Plan, in Plain English

✓ Lead with the quality of the meat

The thing that sets you apart is the quality, so we put it front and centre. Close-ups of the cuts, the marbling, the freshness, and the honest story of why your meat beats a supermarket tray. Quality is your strongest card, and it happens to film beautifully.

✓ Show off the range of cuts

You carry a serious range, and every cut is its own piece of content: what it is, what it is best for, and how to cook it. That is an almost endless content bank, and it quietly positions Prime Meats as the place that knows meat properly.

✓ Put your knowledge on camera

You and Jordan know this trade inside out, and that is gold on social. Education content, why our meat is better, how to cook the perfect steak, which cut for which dish, builds trust faster than anything else and is exactly the kind of thing people save and share.

✓ Grow the wholesale and restaurant side

A big goal for you is getting more restaurants and venues buying, and content is a genuine sales tool for exactly that. We feature the wholesale side properly: big orders being prepped, the quality and scale you can supply, the trade you already do. That makes your videos work double duty, winning retail customers through the door and putting Prime Meats in front of new trade accounts across the North East.

✓ Build the brand from day one, including TikTok

Consistent short-form video across Instagram, TikTok and Facebook, filmed in the shop, to grow a local audience that turns into customers. TikTok in particular is where butchers do big numbers, and starting fresh means we build it the right way from the first post.
