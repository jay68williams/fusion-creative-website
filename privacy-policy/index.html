Privacy Policy

Fusion Creative App (Instagram Integration) — Last updated: 27 April 2026

This privacy policy explains how Fusion Creative ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects data obtained through the Meta (Instagram) API when you connect your Instagram Business account to our platform.

1. Who we are

Fusion Creative is a short-form video and marketing agency based in Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom. We build analytics dashboards and content tools for our clients' Instagram Business accounts.

Contact: hello@fusioncreative.uk

2. What data we collect

When you connect your Instagram Business account through Meta's API, we collect and store the following:

Data type Examples
Business profile information Username, display name, profile picture URL, biography, follower count, following count, media count
Post and media data Post captions, media URLs, timestamps, media type (image, video, carousel)
Engagement metrics Likes, comments, shares, saves, reach, impressions, story interactions
Access tokens OAuth tokens issued by Meta to authorise API access on your behalf

We do not collect private messages, personal contact details of your followers, or any data from personal (non-business) Instagram accounts.

3. Why we collect this data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Analytics dashboards -- to display your account performance, follower growth, and engagement metrics in your client dashboard
  • Content repurposing -- to help you identify top-performing content and repurpose it across platforms
  • Reporting -- to generate performance reports for your business
  • Service improvement -- to improve the tools and features we offer to clients

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling beyond the services you have engaged us to provide.

4. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following bases:

  • Consent -- you explicitly authorise the connection when you log in via Meta and grant permissions
  • Legitimate interest -- to deliver the analytics and content services you have contracted us to provide
  • Contractual necessity -- processing is required to fulfil our service agreement with you

5. How your data is stored

  • Database: Your profile data, posts, and metrics are stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU). Access is restricted to authenticated users and our internal systems.
  • Access tokens: OAuth tokens from Meta are stored encrypted. They are never exposed in client-side code or logs.
  • Hosting: Our dashboard and website are hosted on Vercel. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access control: Only authorised team members can access the database. Row-level security policies restrict each client to their own data.

6. Third-party services

We share data with the following third-party services, strictly as required to deliver our product:

Service Purpose Data shared
Meta / Instagram API Fetching your business profile, posts, and metrics OAuth tokens (sent to Meta to authenticate requests)
Supabase Database and authentication Profile data, posts, metrics, encrypted tokens
Vercel Web hosting and deployment No personal data stored; serves the front-end application

We do not share your Instagram data with any other third parties.

7. Data retention

  • Your Instagram data is retained for as long as your account is connected and you remain an active client.
  • If you disconnect your Instagram account, we revoke the associated access token immediately and delete your stored Instagram data within 30 days.
  • If your client agreement with us ends, we delete all associated Instagram data within 30 days of termination unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Data deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by:

  1. Emailing hello@fusioncreative.uk with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Disconnecting your Instagram account from our platform (this automatically revokes the access token)

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove all your Instagram-related data from our systems within 14 days and confirm by email once complete.

You can also remove our app's access directly from your Instagram settings under Settings > Website Permissions > Apps and Websites.

9. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access -- request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification -- request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure -- request deletion of your data
  • Restriction -- request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability -- receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object -- object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent -- withdraw your consent at any time by disconnecting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@fusioncreative.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of access tokens
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Restricted access to production systems

No system is completely secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by UK GDPR.

11. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for business users. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor's data has been submitted, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at fusioncreative.uk/privacy-policy. Material changes will be communicated by email to connected users.
