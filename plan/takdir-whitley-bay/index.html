Step 1 - Setup
Three Quick Things Before We Start

A few small admin jobs need ticking off so the content has the best foundation to perform.

Action

Launch a TikTok account for Takdir

You do not have a TikTok presence yet. Indian food is one of the highest-performing categories on TikTok and the under-35 booking crowd discovers restaurants there now. We will set this up under your handle and post the same content as Instagram.

Action

Switch the Instagram account to a Professional / Business account

Quick free switch in Instagram Settings. Unlocks insights, analytics, action buttons (Reserve, Order, Call), and ad capability. Currently set up as a personal account which is leaving a lot of growth on the table.

Keep

Website is good as it is for now

The site functions well and converts traffic. We will leave it as is for the first phase. Down the line we may suggest a few visual tweaks, but no rebuild needed for now. Focus stays on content.

Step 3 - Content Pillars
What We Will Be Filming

Every reel falls into one of these four pillars. The mix keeps the feed varied and gives the algorithm different reasons to push your content to different audiences.

1

Best Sellers

The dishes you sell the most of, filmed properly. People order what they see. We highlight the heroes that already have proof of demand.

2

The Location

The seafront, the sea views, the Whitley Bay coast. One of the most photogenic restaurant settings in the North East. This is what makes Takdir share-worthy beyond the food.

3

Offers and Deals

Any specials, banquet menus, lunch deals, or seasonal offers. Value-reveal content drives bookings faster than anything else.

4

Unique / Signature Dishes

The dishes you do that nobody else does. The buttered honey chicken people review about. Anything regional or off-menu that gives Takdir its own identity.

Step 5 - Entertainment Content
Why We Do Not Just Sell, We Entertain

Selling content does not perform. People scroll past adverts. The restaurants going viral right now are the ones that entertain first and sell second. The food is still the star, but the reels feel like content people want to watch, not ads they want to skip.

This is the difference between a "trendy" restaurant and a regular one online. Trendy restaurants are watched. Regular ones are scrolled past. The accounts below are doing this brilliantly and we will be using them as creative reference for Takdir.
