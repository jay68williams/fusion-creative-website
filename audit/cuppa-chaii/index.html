What Is Already Working Well
Credit Where It Is Due

✓ The brand and the momentum are real

Nine sites and counting, a Great Taste Award, and a franchise model expanding fast. Most businesses we audit are trying to get off the ground. You are scaling. That changes the job from "build an audience" to "turn this momentum into reach", which is a much better problem to have.

✓ You are already using trends

The Elmo meme, the Gen Z humour, "in our sweet era", pink lemonade Wednesdays. Most cafe accounts post a flat lat and a price. You are already speaking the language of the feed, which is exactly why your better reels break a few thousand views. The instinct is there. It just needs to be consistent.

✓ The heritage story is gold

A grandmother's recipe from Nazimabad, Karachi, brought to the British high street. That is a story people feel, and your Karachi posts are some of your best performers, the Nazimabad reel did nearly 9,600 views. Authenticity and nostalgia travel, and you have both in abundance.

✓ The experience is built for content

Chaii, doughnuts, board games, carrom, comedy nights, community events. This is not just a drink, it is a place people spend their evening. That is endless content: the games, the crowd, the karak comedy, the soft serve. Most cafes would kill for this much to film.

✓ Your store openings already pop

The Purley "now open" reel did around 8,600 views with 192 likes and 25 comments, your strongest recent post. That is the proof: a launch moment, filmed right, outperforms everything. With a store opening every few weeks, that is a repeatable viral format you are sitting on.

Where The Opportunity Is
Five Areas With Real Potential

Cuppa' Chaii already has the brand, the product, the trends instinct and the momentum. The gap is consistency and reach: turning a feed that sometimes does 9,000 views and sometimes does 400 into one that reliably travels. These are the five areas where focused effort would do it.

1

Consistency Is the Real Unlock

Your reels swing from nearly 10,000 views to under 500. The hits are there, the floor is the problem. The accounts that grow fast do not get lucky once a month, they post hooky, trend-led, reaction content several times a week, every week, so the algorithm always has something to push.

We would build a consistent weekly engine of reaction, trend and heritage content, so your floor rises and the hits come more often. This is the single biggest lever, and you already have the raw material across nine sites.

2

Turn Every Store Opening Into a Viral Moment

Your Purley launch reel was your best post. You have a store opening every few weeks, and right now each launch is a one-off. It should be a system: a build-up series, the queue, the first pour, the reaction, every single time. Done right, each opening becomes a piece of content that fills the new store and sells the next franchise.

For a brand expanding this fast, this alone could be transformational. The openings are happening anyway. We just make sure each one travels.

3

Lead With the Pour, the Reaction and the Heritage

"They said it's just chaii. I said you've never had Cuppa' Chaii." You already wrote that line, it is perfect. The chaii pour is pure sensory content: the steam, the stretch, the karak. Pair that with real reactions, first sips, the dhaba story, and you have the most shareable content in the category.

Reaction plus heritage plus a satisfying pour is exactly what the For You page rewards, and it sells the experience far better than a flat product shot.

4

Run It as a Brand and a Network of Locations

A nine-site franchise is a content advantage, not a complication. The main @cuppachaiiuk account carries the hero brand content and the trends, while each location feeds in local moments: the Purley comedy night, the Manchester crowd, the Birmingham launch. More sites means more raw footage and more local reach, all pointing back to the brand.

We would set up a simple system so every store knows what to film, and we turn it into polished content that grows both the brand and the local store.

5

Scale TikTok, and Put Spend Behind Openings

TikTok is the single best platform for the reaction-led, trend-led, food content you already make, and it is where a brand like this can break out nationally. Every reel should be cut for TikTok and Shorts too, for free extra reach.

And because you open stores on fixed dates, a small, well-targeted ad budget around each launch puts your best opening reel in front of everyone nearby, filling the store from day one. For a business opening this often, that pays for itself fast.

Digital Enhancements
Beyond the Main Feed

A few extra wins that would complement the content work for a brand expanding this fast.

Scale TikTok and Shorts

TikTok is where a brand like Cuppa' Chaii can break out nationally, and it rewards exactly the reaction and trend content you already make. Every reel should be cut for TikTok and YouTube Shorts too, for free extra reach.

  • Post the reaction, pour and trend content to TikTok consistently, not just Instagram
  • Cross-post every reel to TikTok and YouTube Shorts
  • Use city and location tags so each post reaches the right local audience
  • Lean into the formats that over-perform on TikTok: ASMR, reaction, trends

A Simple Content System Across Every Store

With nine sites and growing, the footage is everywhere, it just needs capturing. A simple shot-list and posting system means every location knows what to film, and we turn it into polished brand content.

  • A quick what-to-film guide for each store, so nothing is missed
  • The brand account carries hero content; locations feed in local moments
  • A repeatable opening series for every new franchise store
  • Monthly reporting on reach, growth and best-performing content

Run Ads Around Every Launch

Because you open stores on fixed dates, paid ads are a near-guaranteed win: put your best opening reel in front of everyone nearby in the run-up, and the store opens to a queue.

  • Target the local area around each new site in the weeks before opening
  • Use your best opening and reaction reels as the ads
  • Retarget anyone who engages but does not visit
  • Track cost per visit so the spend is always accountable

Why it fits Cuppa' Chaii: For a franchise opening this often, a launch always has a fixed date and a fixed catchment. That makes it the easiest, highest-return ad spend there is.
