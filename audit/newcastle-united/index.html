The Audit

What We Noticed

Football content is world-class. Match highlights, player access, behind-the-scenes from the dressing room. The football side of your social presence is professional, consistent, and well-executed.
Commercial content is the gap. St James' Park events, kit launches, hospitality experiences, sponsor activations. The non-football side of the brand is largely untouched on social. That is where the revenue opportunity lives.
The women's team is growing fast. @newcastleunitedwomen has 112K followers and the momentum is building, but the social content strategy is still early-stage. Dedicated short-form content could accelerate that growth significantly.
YouTube is underperforming. 912K subscribers for a Premier League club with this fanbase is low. A dedicated Shorts strategy would drive subscriber growth without requiring new long-form production resources.
Reels are mostly repurposed match footage. The current short-form output leans heavily on match clips rather than native Reels and TikTok creative. For the commercial side, that format gap is costing reach and engagement.
The Strategy

The Opportunity

01

Commercial & Venue Content

St James' Park is a 4,500-capacity conference and events venue, a hospitality destination, and a stage for sponsor activations and kit launches. None of that is being sold through short-form video. We would create native Reels, TikToks, and Shorts that position the non-football side of Newcastle United as a premium brand in its own right.

02

Women's Team Growth

As the women's team pushes for WSL promotion, the audience is there and growing. Dedicated social content, personality-driven Reels, matchday coverage, community engagement, would build the brand at the pace the team deserves. Not repurposed clips. Native content made for the platform.

03

YouTube Shorts Strategy

912K subscribers should be 1.5M+. YouTube Shorts is the fastest path to get there. The production quality already exists across your other channels. We would bring a dedicated short-form content strategy to YouTube that converts casual viewers into subscribers at scale.
