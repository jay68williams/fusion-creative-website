An honest look at your content

Award-winning, but under-scaled

Two-time Motor Trader Best Social Media winners. Your team clearly knows what good looks like. But 31,000 followers spread across 198 dealerships works out to roughly 158 per location. For a brand turning over billions, social reach has not kept pace with revenue.

Content leans corporate

The feed is polished but promotional. Press releases, model launches, corporate milestones. These perform well internally but do not stop thumbs on TikTok or Reels. The content that drives reach in 2026 is personality-driven, raw, and built for the algorithm.

TikTok has viral signals

A single Ford Cortina clip pulled 175K reach. That is proof the audience is there when the content is right. But posting is inconsistent and there is no clear content strategy driving the account forward. Virality should not be accidental.

YouTube is nascent

Hiring a YouTube Channel Lead tells us the channel is not established yet. That is not a weakness. It is a window. Launching with professional short-form and episodic content from day one means you skip the amateur phase entirely.

No local content strategy

Vertu has dealerships across the North East but no dedicated local content for Newcastle, Sunderland, or Gateshead. Local content drives footfall. A national brand with a local voice is how you win both the algorithm and the customer.

Content Concepts

5 videos we would make this month

01

First Keys

Emotional customer collection moments filmed cinematic. The handshake, the first sit, the drive off the lot. People share these because they feel something.

02

Under the Hood

Your workshop team showing what they actually do. Satisfying detail shots, brake changes, engine rebuilds. The auto-detail niche on TikTok pulls hundreds of millions of views.

03

The Showroom Tour

ASMR-style walkaround of new arrivals. Leather seats, door close sounds, dashboard glow. Slow, cinematic, designed for saves and shares.

04

Road Test

Quick review format, 60 seconds, trending audio. First impressions from the driver's seat. Not a car review channel, just honest reactions that feel real.

05

The Team

Staff personalities, their car stories, why they love what they do. People buy from people. Let your team be the reason someone chooses Vertu over the dealership down the road.
