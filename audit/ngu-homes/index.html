The Problem

What We Noticed

NGU is not short on effort. 1,595 posts is serious output. But the results tell a different story. This is not a frequency problem. It is a content strategy problem.

  • 1,595 posts but only 729 followers. The content is not resonating with the algorithm or the audience. That ratio should be inverted.
  • Reels are text overlays and slideshows, not video with real humans in them. Instagram and TikTok are pushing video with faces. Static graphics get buried.
  • No face-to-camera content from Chris, Nigel or any of the team. Property audiences trust people, not branded templates.
  • Market data content is genuinely useful and well-researched, but it is delivered in a format nobody engages with. The substance is there. The format is wrong.
  • Zero TikTok presence in a massive property TikTok space. Letting agents, property investors and relocation content performs extremely well on the platform.
  • No dedicated marketing person listed on the team page. Content is likely being produced in-house around other responsibilities.
  • Engagement sits at near zero across recent posts. Most are getting 0 to 5 likes with no meaningful comments or shares.

The Opportunity

Three Ways to Fix This

1

Founder-Led Content

Chris and Nigel have 17+ years of property expertise between them. That is the content. Put them on camera sharing market insights, landlord tips and honest takes on the Gateshead and Newcastle property market. Property audiences trust people, not graphics. Founder-led accounts consistently outperform brand pages across every metric.

2

Property Tours That Stop the Scroll

Cinematic walkthrough Reels and TikToks of your listings. Audio trends, smooth transitions, lifestyle-driven shots that show what it feels like to live there. Not a slideshow of photos from Rightmove. These are the most shareable and saveable format in property content right now.

3

Investor Education Series

You are already publishing market data. That is valuable content most agencies do not have. Turn those posts into talking-head Reels where Chris or Nigel walk through the numbers on camera. Same substance, 10x the engagement. Position NGU as the authority on North East property investment.

Content That Converts

5 Video Ideas for NGU

Idea 01
"Before and After"
Property refurbishment transformations. NGU handles refurbs and this is gold for short-form video. A quick before shot, a time-lapse or transition, and the finished result. These go viral in the property space because the transformation is inherently satisfying to watch.
Idea 02
"Market Minute"
60-second market update, face-to-camera, data on screen. Chris or Nigel delivering the key stat of the week in plain English. No jargon, no static infographic. This positions NGU as the go-to source for North East property insight and builds trust with landlords considering switching agents.
Idea 03
"Sold in 7 Days"
The story behind a property that sold fast. What made it work, how it was priced, what the strategy was. Social proof that NGU delivers results. Potential vendors watching this will think "I want that for my property."
Idea 04
"Tenant Wins"
Happy tenant moments. Key handovers, moving-in clips, reactions to a new place. Humanises the brand, shows NGU cares about the people not just the properties, and creates shareable content that tenants post to their own stories and feeds.
Idea 05
"The Brothers"
Chris and Nigel's story. How they built NGU from scratch, what drives them, the reality of running a property company in the North East. Founder story content builds deep audience connection and makes NGU more than just another lettings brand. People follow people.
