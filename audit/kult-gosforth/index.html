Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Today

These are formats that are working right now for cafes and bars on TikTok and Instagram. All of them play to KULT's strengths as a dual-concept venue with incredible visual appeal.

Idea 01
"Same place. Two vibes."
Transition Brand Story Viral Format

Split screen or transition showing KULT by day (bright, matcha, protein bowls, coffee) versus KULT by night (cocktails, wine, moody lighting). Quick cuts synced to music. The contrast tells the whole story in 15 seconds.

Why it works: This format is massive on TikTok and Reels. The before/after transformation triggers curiosity and shares. People tag friends, save it for later, and comment "we need to go here." It is the single best way to communicate the dual identity that makes KULT unique.
Idea 02
"What protein looks like at KULT"
Food Content Satisfying Health

Close-up of an acai bowl or protein bowl being assembled. Toppings falling, drizzles, the finished product from above. Satisfying, shareable, saves-worthy. No voiceover needed, just the sounds and a trending audio.

Why it works: Food assembly content is one of the highest-performing categories on TikTok. Acai bowls and protein bowls especially have a dedicated audience that actively searches for this content. It drives saves (people bookmark it), shares (people tag friends), and location searches ("where is this?").
Idea 03
"Gosforth's best kept secret"
Discovery Local Interior Reveal

Walk down Gosforth High Street, find KULT, push open the door, reveal the interior. The discovery format drives curiosity and shares. Show the space, the menu board, a coffee being made, the atmosphere.

Why it works: The "hidden gem" discovery format is one of the most reliable viral formats for local businesses. It triggers the "I live near here and I did not know about this" response, which drives comments, shares, and immediate visits. Gosforth locals will share this with everyone they know.
Idea 04
"Your morning matcha at KULT"
Matcha ASMR Routine

The matcha pour, the latte art, the first sip. Close-up, slow motion, satisfying. Matcha content has a huge dedicated audience on TikTok and Reels. This positions KULT as the matcha destination in Gosforth.

Why it works: Matcha content has its own massive community on TikTok. People actively search "matcha near me" and "best matcha Newcastle." Owning this category in Gosforth with consistent, beautiful matcha content means every time someone searches, KULT shows up. It is a long-term discovery engine.
Idea 05
"Friday night at KULT"
Evening Cocktails Atmosphere

Show the cafe-bar evening side. Cocktails being made, wine being poured, the atmosphere shifting. Moody lighting, clinking glasses, music. People do not know about this side of the business. One video changes that.

Why it works: Most people who walk past KULT during the day assume it is just a cafe. Showing the evening transformation surprises people and doubles your potential audience. The cocktail-making content performs brilliantly on its own, and combining it with "you thought this was just a cafe" is a powerful hook.

Fix the Website and Make It the Hub

kultgosforth.com needs to be working and it needs to be the central destination for everything about KULT. Right now, anyone who finds the link from the High Life North article, a Google search, or social media hits a broken page.

  • Daytime menu (coffee, matcha, protein bowls, acai, lunch) and evening menu (cocktails, wine, beer)

  • The KULT story - the concept, the team, why it exists
  • Events calendar and event space booking information

Priority: This is the single most important fix. Every other marketing effort sends people to the website eventually. If it is not working, everything else is undermined.

Set Up Google Business and TripAdvisor Immediately

These two platforms are how locals discover new places. Without them, KULT is invisible to anyone searching "cafes in Gosforth," "cocktail bars Gosforth," or "brunch near me." Setting them up takes an afternoon but the impact is permanent.

  • Google Business Profile with professional photos, both sets of opening hours, menu highlights, and the correct website link
  • TripAdvisor listing to capture the dining and evening crowd

  • In a competitive Gosforth High Street, reviews drive discovery - get ahead of this early
