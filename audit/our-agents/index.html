Marketing the location and the lifestyle, not only the bricks, is exactly the instinct that wins on social. Northumberland sells itself. Lean into that and it pulls in both buyers and the sellers watching to see who markets a home best.

Where The Opportunity Is
Six Things We Would Focus On

You already have the brand, the proposition and the results. The job is to turn that into reach and instructions: get in front of Northumberland homeowners thinking of selling, and make Our Agents the name they already trust before they pick up the phone. These are the six areas we would focus on.

1

Make Every Launch a Content Moment

Your whole promise is that you launch homes, you do not just list them. So let people see it. The reveal reel, the walkthrough, the first look, the open-house buzz, the queue at the door. A new instruction should produce a piece of content that shows off exactly why a seller would want you marketing their home.

Every launch is a ready-made story, and it is the single best advert for winning the next instruction.

2

Use Social as a Sales Channel, Not Just an Advert

You do not only have to talk about how well you sell. You can let the selling do the talking. Showcase the homes themselves, the standout kitchen, the view, the garden in the sun, presented properly as short video, so the listing markets the property to buyers and helps it sell faster. The marketing of the house becomes the content.

And it doubles as your strongest pull for new instructions. When a homeowner watches you make someone else's house look incredible and sell quickly, they do not need a sales pitch. They think, that is how I want my home marketed. The listing content becomes the lead generator.

3

Sell the Postcode, Not Just the Property

"Thinking of moving to Morpeth?" "Five things you only know if you live in Northumberland." Area and lifestyle guides pull in buyers, but more importantly they are watched by the local homeowners who will one day be your sellers. It positions you as the local expert, not just another agent with a board outside.

This is content only a genuinely local agency can make, and it is exactly where you can out-market the big portals and the national chains.

4

Turn Your Results Into Proof Reels

"Sold in 11 days." "Sold at open house for over asking." Your track record is your best marketing, and right now it is hidden in stats rather than shown as content. Short, punchy result reels and quick vendor testimonials are the most persuasive thing an agency can post, because they answer the only question a seller really has: can you actually sell my home, fast.

Proof travels, and it speaks straight to the homeowner weighing up who to instruct.

5

Put Jonathan and the Team on Camera

People do not instruct a logo, they instruct a person they trust. The most effective estate agency content right now is agent-led: faces, personality, the human side of guiding someone through one of the most stressful moves of their life. Your no-nonsense, available-when-others-have-clocked-off approach is a story in itself.

Personality-led content builds familiarity, and familiarity is what makes a seller pick you over the agent they have never seen.

6

Reels and TikTok for Reach, Plus Targeted Local Ads

Nearly 800 posts and a small following tells you the effort is right but the format is not. Short-form video built to travel, with proper hooks rather than listing carousels, is what earns reach. Pair that with a small, tightly targeted ad budget aimed at Northumberland homeowners, and you put your best launch and result content in front of people actually thinking about selling.

For an estate agency, one extra instruction pays for a lot of content and ads, which makes this one of the highest-return moves you can make.

Ready-Made Content
4 Video Ideas You Could Film Around Your Next Launch

These are formats working right now on TikTok and Instagram Reels, built around what you already do: the launch, the result, the place and the people. Each one could be filmed on a phone around a single instruction.

Idea 01
"This is how you launch a home"
LaunchRevealWalkthrough

A fast, cinematic launch reel of a new instruction: the reveal, the best rooms, the view, the open-house buzz. Strong hook on frame one ("Northumberland, meet your next home"). Trending audio. Under 25 seconds. The on-camera version of your "we launch them" promise.

Why it works: It shows a seller exactly what they get with you, and a great launch reel is the best advert there is for winning the next instruction.
Idea 02
"Sold in 11 days. Here's how."
ProofResultTrust

A quick result story: the home, the launch, the open house, the sold board, the happy vendor. Lead with the number ("sold in 11 days, here is exactly how"). A few seconds of the journey, end on the result and a smiling seller.

Why it works: It answers the only question a seller has, can you sell my home fast, with proof rather than a promise. The most persuasive content an agency can post.
Idea 03
"Thinking of moving to Morpeth?"
LocalLifestyleArea guide

A lifestyle guide to a Northumberland town: the high street, the schools, the walks, the coffee spots, what it is really like to live there. Helpful, watchable, and built to be saved and shared. Tag the location. Not a sales pitch, a love letter to the place.

Why it works: It reaches the local homeowners who are your future sellers and positions you as the area expert, content only a genuinely local agency can make.
Idea 04
"Why we don't get paid until your house sells"
FounderPOVProposition

Jonathan to camera, straight talking, explaining the no sale no fee model and why you back yourselves. Honest, confident, a little bit against-the-grain of the typical agent. Strong hook ("most agents get paid whether they sell your home or not. We don't.").

Why it works: People instruct people. A founder explaining a genuinely different model builds trust fast, and it makes the proposition impossible to forget.

When a homeowner decides to sell, they search and they read reviews before they ever call. Your Google profile and reviews are doing quiet, heavy lifting, and they deserve to be front and centre and constantly fed.

Content That Also Recruits Agents

You are building a self-employed agent model under the Our Agents brand, with Jonathan as the mentor. The same content engine that wins instructions also attracts the right agents, by showing the culture, the wins and what it is like to build a business with you.
