What Is Already Working Well
Credit Where It Is Due

✓ The concept is built for social media

Cake decorating is one of the most naturally shareable activities on TikTok and Instagram. The process is satisfying to watch, the finished cakes are colourful and photogenic, and every single customer creates something unique. Most businesses have to work hard to make their product look good on camera. Yours does it by default.

✓ The Metrocentre location is a huge advantage

You are inside one of the biggest shopping centres in the North East, which means high footfall, easy access for families, and a built-in audience of people already out looking for something to do. That location alone gives you a head start most small businesses would love.

✓ Fresh daily baking shows real quality

The fact that the cakes are baked fresh on-site every day is a proper differentiator. It signals quality and care, and it gives you a daily behind-the-scenes content moment that most experience businesses simply do not have.

✓ You are already planning smart expansions

Parents and Tots sessions, evening events, at-home kits. These show you are thinking beyond the core product, and each new format is a fresh content angle and a new audience to reach.

✓ The website and booking flow are clean

The site is straightforward, the booking process is clear, and the group discount structure encourages bigger bookings. That is a solid foundation to send traffic to.

Where The Opportunity Is
Five Things We Would Focus On

You have a concept that is made for short-form video and a location that puts you in front of thousands of people every day. The job now is to turn that into a steady stream of content that fills your sessions, builds a following and makes Sprinkle Studio the experience everyone in the North East knows about. These are the five areas we would focus on.

1

Short-Form Video of the Decorating Process

This is the single biggest lever. Cake decorating videos are one of the most watched categories on TikTok and Reels because the process itself is satisfying: piping, smoothing, choosing sprinkles, the before and after reveal. Every session produces content, you just need to capture it the right way.

A steady stream of 15 to 30 second process videos, well shot and well edited with a strong hook on frame one, is what turns a small local page into something that travels.

2

Turn Every Customer Into a Content Moment

Every person who walks through the door is decorating a unique cake. That is a constant supply of fresh, original content that never runs out. With permission, you can capture the best creations, the funniest reactions, the proudest reveals, and turn them into reels that feel authentic and relatable.

User-generated content and reaction videos perform brilliantly in this space, and they do double duty: they entertain and they show a potential customer exactly what the experience looks and feels like.

3

Build a TikTok Presence From Day One

TikTok is where cake decorating content gets the most organic reach, and right now Sprinkle Studio does not appear to have an established TikTok account. That is a gap worth closing quickly, because TikTok rewards new accounts that post consistently, and your content type is exactly what the algorithm pushes.

A dedicated TikTok account, posting three to five times a week with process videos, customer reactions and behind-the-scenes clips, could build a meaningful local following within the first few months.

4

Use the "Fresh Daily" Story

Baking fresh every morning is something most competitors cannot claim. That daily routine is a content series in itself: the early morning prep, the batter going in, the cakes cooling, the crumb-coated blanks lined up and ready. It humanises the brand, shows the care behind the product and gives you a recurring, reliable posting format.

Think of it as a daily "open" moment that invites people into the studio before the first customer arrives.

5

Targeted Local Ads to Fill Sessions

Organic reach builds the brand, but a small, tightly targeted ad budget puts your best content in front of the people most likely to book: families, couples, friend groups and hen parties within driving distance of the Metrocentre. Your best-performing reels become the ads, which means the creative is already proven before you spend a penny on it.

For an experience business, even a modest ad spend can move the needle on midweek and off-peak sessions, which is usually where the empty slots sit.

Ready-Made Content
4 Video Ideas You Could Film This Week

These are formats working right now on TikTok and Instagram Reels, built around what you already do: the baking, the decorating, the reactions and the finished cakes. Each one could be filmed on a phone during a normal day at the studio.

Idea 01
"Watch me decorate my first ever cake"
POVProcessSatisfying

A first-person angle of a customer (or you) decorating a bento cake from start to finish: choosing the colours, piping the frosting, picking the sprinkles, the finished reveal. Trending audio, quick cuts, under 30 seconds. The kind of satisfying process video that stops people mid-scroll.

Why it works: Process and ASMR-style decorating videos are among the most viewed categories on TikTok. It shows exactly what the experience feels like and makes people want to try it.
Idea 02
"We bake 50 cakes before you even arrive"
Behind the scenesDaily routineTrust

A quick morning montage: the early alarm, the batter mixing, the ovens, the crumb-coated cakes lined up ready for the day. Strong hook ("most cake decorating places use pre-made bases, we bake ours fresh every single morning"). Under 20 seconds.

Why it works: Behind-the-scenes content builds trust and separates you from competitors. The "fresh daily" angle is a genuine differentiator worth showing off.
Idea 03
"Her face when she saw the finished cake"
ReactionEmotionalShareable

A quick reveal reaction: a child, a friend, a hen party seeing their finished cake for the first time. Capture the genuine delight. These moments happen every session, you just need a phone ready to film them (with permission). Lead with the reaction, show the cake after.

Why it works: Reaction and reveal videos are some of the most shared content on social media. Real, unscripted joy is impossible to fake, and it makes the viewer want to create that moment for themselves.
Idea 04
"Date night, but make it cake"
CouplesLifestyleDate idea

Position Sprinkle Studio as a unique date night. Two people decorating side by side, comparing their creations, laughing at the results. Tag it as a date idea for the Metrocentre. Quick, fun, lifestyle-led. Think "things to do in Gateshead" and "date ideas North East".

Why it works: "Date idea" and "things to do" are high-search categories on TikTok and Instagram. Positioning the studio as a date night widens your audience beyond families and friend groups.
Digital Enhancements
Beyond the Main Feed

A few extra wins that would compound the content work and help turn more of your visibility into booked sessions.

Google Business Profile and Reviews

When someone searches "things to do at the Metrocentre" or "cake decorating Gateshead", your Google profile is often the first thing they see. Getting it fully set up, posting regularly and actively collecting reviews from happy customers will build trust and improve your local search visibility at the same time.

  • Optimise the profile for "cake decorating Gateshead" and "Metrocentre activities"
  • Encourage every happy customer to leave a Google review
  • Add photos and updates regularly
  • Turn your best reviews into shareable content

Seasonal and Event-Led Content

Cake decorating is naturally seasonal. Halloween, Christmas, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Easter, school holidays. Each one is a chance to create themed sessions and themed content that reaches people searching for gift ideas, activities and things to do during the holidays.

  • Plan themed sessions around major holidays and school breaks
  • Create content in advance so it catches the search wave early
  • Promote gift vouchers as a low-effort, high-margin product

Hen Parties, Team Days and Private Events

Group bookings are higher value and bring in audiences who might never have searched for cake decorating on their own. A dedicated push for hen parties, birthday parties, team-building days and corporate events, with content and landing pages built around each, could fill a lot of midweek and evening slots.

  • Create short content showing real group events at the studio
  • Target hen party and team day searches with local ads
  • Make group booking easy and visible on the website

Why it matters: A single hen party or team day fills more seats than individual walk-ins, and groups share more content from the experience, giving you free reach with every event.
