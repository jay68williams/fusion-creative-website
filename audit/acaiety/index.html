TikTok Is Made for Acaiety

Acai bowl content is massive on TikTok. The satisfying swirl of soft-serve acai, the topping assembly, the first bite. These are the kinds of videos that get millions of views. Review Listings Could Build Huge Trust

There is no Google Business listing or TripAdvisor page for Acaiety yet. This is completely understandable for a new business, but getting these set up early is one of the highest-impact things you can do. When someone in Jesmond searches "acai bowls near me" or "cafe Jesmond," Google shows businesses with active listings first.

A Google Business listing is free and takes minutes to set up. Once it is live, it starts collecting reviews, showing your hours, displaying your location on maps, and appearing in local search results. Even a handful of early reviews will make a big difference. Your customers are clearly enjoying the experience. Giving them an easy way to share that publicly will compound over time.

Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Today

These are formats that are working right now for health-focused cafes and aesthetic food brands on TikTok and Instagram. All of them play to Acaiety's strengths as a unique acai and vintage concept in Jesmond.

Idea 01
"How we build the perfect acai bowl"
Process Satisfying Food

Overhead camera. Start with an empty bowl. Soft-serve acai goes in. Then toppings, one by one: granola, fruit, drizzle, the final garnish. Each step a quick cut. Satisfying, colourful, trending audio. End with the finished bowl and someone taking the first spoonful.

Why it works: Bowl-building content is one of the most shared food formats on TikTok and Instagram. The layering process is inherently satisfying to watch, and the colourful end result makes people stop scrolling. This is the kind of video that drives "where is this?" comments and gets saved for later visits.
Idea 02
"This cafe has a secret vintage store inside"
Discovery Concept Reveal

Walk into Acaiety. Show the cafe, the bowls, the menu. Then reveal: "But wait, there is more." Walk through to The Hideaway Store. Show the vintage rails, the pieces, the vibe. The reveal format creates surprise and keeps people watching until the end. Caption: "Acai bowls and vintage fashion. Only in Jesmond."

Why it works: Reveal and discovery content is TikTok gold. The "hidden gem" format combined with a genuinely surprising dual concept creates the perfect hook. People share this kind of content because it feels like insider knowledge. It also communicates your full offering in one short video.
Idea 03
"Morning matcha at Acaiety"
Aesthetic Lifestyle Drink

A simple, aesthetic video. The matcha being prepared. The pour. The latte art or the swirl. The first sip. Morning light in the cafe. Soft, calming audio. No voiceover needed. Just pure visual satisfaction. This is the kind of content that defines a brand aesthetic.

Why it works: Matcha content has a massive audience on TikTok and Instagram. The green colour pops on screen, and the preparation process is hypnotic. This positions Acaiety as a lifestyle brand, not just a cafe. It attracts the exact demographic you want: health-conscious, aesthetic-driven, socially active.
Idea 04
"Acai bowl or vintage shopping? Why not both?"
Dual Concept Fun Relatable

A fun, fast-paced video that cuts between two experiences in one visit. Ordering a bowl. Browsing the vintage rails. Taking a bite. Trying on a jacket. The smoothie arriving. Finding the perfect piece. End with the customer leaving with a bag and a smile. "The only place you can do both."

Why it works: This format highlights what makes Acaiety completely different from every other cafe in Jesmond. It is entertaining, shareable, and it communicates the full experience in under 30 seconds. People tag friends in this kind of content because it feels like the perfect outing.
Idea 05
"The soft-serve swirl that broke the internet"
Close-Up Satisfying Hook

Extreme close-up of the soft-serve acai being dispensed. Slow motion. The swirl building. The perfect peak. Then back to real speed as toppings are added. Keep it short, 15 seconds maximum. Use a trending audio that builds. The kind of video people watch three times.

Why it works: Short, satisfying food videos are the bread and butter of TikTok virality. The soft-serve swirl is visually perfect for this format. It is the kind of content that gets millions of views because it is effortlessly rewatchable. It also makes people immediately want to visit.
Digital Enhancements
Beyond Social Media

These are strategic changes that would make a real difference alongside the social media work.

Set Up Google Business and TripAdvisor

Getting listed on Google and TripAdvisor is one of the highest-impact, lowest-effort things you can do right now. It is free, it takes minutes, and it immediately puts Acaiety on the map for anyone searching "cafe Jesmond," "acai bowls Newcastle," or "brunch Acorn Road."

  • Create a Google Business profile with photos, hours, menu highlights, and the correct address
  • Claim or create a TripAdvisor listing
  • Encourage early customers to leave reviews to build momentum
  • Respond to every review to show you are active and engaged

Quick win: Even 10-15 Google reviews at 4.5+ stars would put Acaiety on the map for local searches in Jesmond. Your customers are already enjoying the experience. Give them an easy way to share it.

Upgrade the Website

acaiety.co.uk is live, which is a great start. But there is so much potential to make it a more powerful tool. A full menu with photos, the story behind the concept, The Hideaway Store section, an Instagram feed embed, and clear "visit us" calls to action would transform it.

  • Add professional food photography showcasing the bowls and drinks
  • Tell the story of why you combined acai with vintage fashion
  • Include a dedicated section for The Hideaway Store
  • Embed your Instagram feed so the site always feels fresh and alive

Influencer Collaboration Strategy

Influencers are already visiting organically, which is brilliant. A more structured approach would amplify this. Inviting local food and lifestyle creators for a complimentary visit, providing a branded experience, and reposting their content would build a steady stream of authentic coverage that reaches exactly the audience you want.
