Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Today

These are formats that are performing right now for bars and nightlife venues on TikTok and Instagram. Every single one of them plays to 33 Durham's unique strengths as a speakeasy.

Idea 01
"I found a secret bar in Durham. You need a code to get in."
Discovery Mystery Speakeasy

Walk up to an ordinary-looking door. Pause. Enter the code. The door opens. Reveal the speakeasy interior: the low lighting, the prohibition-era decor, the bar, the cocktails. Let the contrast between the outside and the inside do all the work. End with "33 Durham. Durham's only speakeasy."

Why it works: "Secret bar" and "hidden bar" content is one of the most viral formats in the nightlife space. The reveal moment triggers massive engagement. People save these videos, share them in group chats, and plan visits. This single video format could put 33 Durham on the map for thousands of people who never knew it existed.
Idea 02
"Watch our bartender make the most beautiful cocktail in Durham"
Cocktails Craftsmanship ASMR

Close-up of the bartender's hands. The measure, the shake, the strain, the garnish, the final presentation. Slow, deliberate, satisfying. Let the sounds carry the video: ice clinking, liquid pouring, the shaker. No music needed, or a soft, moody track. End with the finished cocktail in the speakeasy setting.

Why it works: Cocktail ASMR content consistently outperforms other bar content because it is visually and aurally satisfying. It positions 33 Durham as a craft cocktail destination, not just a bar. This type of content gets watched multiple times and shared widely because people find it genuinely enjoyable to watch.
Idea 03
"We booked a cocktail masterclass for my birthday. Here is what happened."
Masterclass Group Experience Bookings

Film a real masterclass group. Arrival, learning the techniques, the first attempts, the laughter, the finished cocktails, the group cheers. Fast cuts, warm energy, fun audio. End with "Book your own masterclass. Link in bio." This is experience marketing at its best.

Why it works: People plan group experiences through social media. When someone sees a group having a great time at a cocktail masterclass, they immediately think about their own birthday, hen party, or work social. This video directly drives bookings and positions the masterclass as a must-do Durham experience.
Idea 04
"POV: 1am at Durham's only speakeasy"
Atmosphere Late Night Vibe

Capture the late-night energy. Dimly lit scenes, cocktails being served, conversations, laughter, the bartender working. Use a moody, atmospheric audio track. No voiceover. Let the vibe speak for itself. This is about selling the feeling of being at 33 Durham late at night.

Why it works: Atmosphere content drives visits more than menu content ever could. People choose bars based on the vibe. A video that captures the energy of 33 Durham at 1am makes viewers feel like they are missing out on something special. That feeling drives bookings for the following weekend.
Idea 05
"The weekly code just dropped. Who is coming tonight?"
Community Exclusivity Engagement

A quick, recurring content format. Each week, tease the new door code on social media. Show the door, show the keypad, hint at what is waiting inside. Drive people to WhatsApp to get the code. This turns your existing marketing mechanic into a weekly content series that builds anticipation and engagement.

Why it works: Recurring content formats build habit and anticipation. Followers start expecting the weekly code drop. It drives engagement (comments asking for the code), grows the WhatsApp list (new followers signing up), and keeps 33 Durham top-of-mind every single week. It turns your most unique feature into your most powerful content tool.
