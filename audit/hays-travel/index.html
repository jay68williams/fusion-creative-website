What We Noticed
Big brand. Quiet feed.

We spent time across your main accounts, branch accounts, job listings, and public ad strategy. Here is what stood out.

98,000 Instagram followers spread across 530+ branches averages roughly 185 followers per branch. For a brand of this scale, organic reach is significantly undertapped.

TikTok content is functional but not scroll-stopping or personality-driven. The format leans informational when the algorithm rewards entertainment and authenticity.

Heavy investment in TV and streaming ad spend with Davina McCall as brand ambassador, but organic social is not receiving the same level of creative attention or budget.

Branch-level social accounts are fragmented and inconsistent. No unified tone, visual identity, or content cadence across the network.

No dedicated content creator or short-form video specialist role is currently being recruited for. The content is being handled alongside other responsibilities.

Recent departures of both the Head of Marketing and Director of Strategy create a natural window for fresh, external creative support without internal politics.

Content Ideas
Five videos we would make first.

01

Pack With Me

A Hays consultant helps a real customer plan and pack for their dream trip. Relatable, useful, and built to be saved and shared. Works across every destination and every branch.

02

Destination in 60

Quick-fire destination showcase with trending audio and cinematic cuts. Sixty seconds of pure wanderlust designed to stop the scroll and drive enquiries.

The Reveal

Genuine customer reactions when they see their surprise holiday booking. Emotional, shareable, and impossible to scroll past. This format goes viral repeatedly in the travel space.
