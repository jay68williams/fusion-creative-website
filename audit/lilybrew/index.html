Where The Opportunity Is
Five Things That Could Change Everything

The product is clearly strong and the brand is beautiful. The gap is between what Lilybrew actually is and how many people in Newcastle know about it. Here is where the biggest opportunities are.

1

Short-Form Video Is the Fastest Way to Grow

Aesthetic cafes are one of the best performing categories on Instagram Reels and TikTok right now. Latte art pours, cake display reveals, brunch plating, cosy interior shots, matcha preparation. This type of content consistently goes viral because people love watching it.

Lilybrew is visually built for this. The clean interior, the floral touches, the colourful drinks and cakes. Every single day in the cafe is content waiting to be captured. A well-shot 15-second reel of a latte pour with the Lilybrew branding visible could reach tens of thousands of people in Newcastle and beyond.

The cafes that are winning on social media right now are not the ones with the biggest budgets. They are the ones posting 4-5 Reels a week of genuinely satisfying, beautiful content. Lilybrew already has the aesthetic. You just need someone capturing it consistently.

2

No Google Business Profile Found

We could not find a Google Business Profile for Lilybrew. This is one of the most important things for any cafe to have. When someone in Jesmond searches "coffee near me" or "best cafe Jesmond" on Google or Google Maps, businesses with a Google Business Profile show up in the map pack at the top of results. Without one, you are invisible to those searches.

Setting up a Google Business Profile is free and takes about 20 minutes. Once it is live, customers can find you on Google Maps, see your hours, read reviews, see photos, and get directions. It is the single most impactful free thing you could do today. It also means your 5-star hygiene rating and customer reviews would start working for you in search results.

3

No TripAdvisor Listing

Similar to Google, we could not find Lilybrew on TripAdvisor. Jesmond gets a lot of visitors, students moving in, parents visiting, tourists exploring Newcastle. TripAdvisor is still one of the first places people look when deciding where to eat or grab coffee in an area they do not know well.

Getting listed on TripAdvisor is also free. With a cafe as well-reviewed as yours clearly is (based on the hygiene rating and the quality of the product), you would start accumulating positive reviews quickly. For a business in Jesmond, this is an easy win that brings in customers who are actively looking for somewhere to go.

4

Your Website Is Missing Key Features

The lilybrew.co.uk website looks clean and has good photography. But when a potential customer lands on it, they cannot see your menu, they cannot order online through your site, and the booking system does not appear to be functional. The "Discover Lilybrew" section has one short paragraph about coffee sourcing but does not tell the full story of what makes the cafe special.

For a cafe, the website menu page is one of the most visited pages. People want to see what you serve, what the prices are, and whether it is worth the visit before they leave the house. Adding a proper menu page, a gallery of your food and drinks, and making the booking URL work would turn your website from a digital brochure into something that actually drives visits.

5

Jesmond Is a Content Goldmine You Are Not Tapping

Jesmond has a young, social, trend-aware audience. Students, young professionals, families. These are people who discover new cafes through Instagram Reels and TikTok, not through traditional advertising. The cafes that dominate in areas like this are the ones that show up in people's feeds consistently.

Right now, when someone searches "Jesmond cafe" or "coffee Jesmond" on TikTok or Instagram, Lilybrew should be the first thing they see. With the right content strategy, you could own that space. Your aesthetic is better than most cafes in the area. The product is clearly there. It is just about getting it in front of people.

Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Tomorrow

These are formats that are working right now for cafes on Instagram and TikTok. All of them use things that are already happening in Lilybrew every day.

Idea 01
"POV: You found the prettiest cafe in Jesmond"
POV Aesthetic Jesmond

Walk-in shot from the street. Push through the door. Pan across the interior. Show the counter, the flowers, the cake display. End on a beautifully made latte being placed on the table. Trending audio. No voiceover needed.

Why it works: "POV: you found" is one of the most viral cafe formats on TikTok and Reels. It lets people imagine themselves discovering you. Aesthetic cafes in this format regularly hit 100K-1M views.
Idea 02
"The perfect latte art pour in 15 seconds"
Satisfying Coffee Loop

Close-up overhead shot of a latte art pour. Slow motion optional. The milk hitting the espresso, the pattern forming. Clean, satisfying, watchable on repeat. These are the videos people save and share.

Why it works: Satisfying coffee content is one of the most saved and shared formats on Instagram. The algorithm loves content that gets saved and replayed. One good latte art reel can outperform a week of static posts.
Idea 03
"I tried the matcha morning deal at Lilybrew and..."
Review Style Matcha Value

Film it like a customer review. Walk in. Order the matcha morning deal. Show the drink being made. Show the dessert of the day. Reveal the price: 7.50 for both. Reaction. Caption: "Why did nobody tell me about this place?"

Why it works: Value reveal content drives comments and shares. When people see a good deal presented well, they tag friends. This positions the matcha mornings deal as a must-try and gives people a reason to visit before 11:30am.
Idea 04
"What our cake display looks like every morning"
Behind the Scenes Baking ASMR

Time-lapse or quick cuts of the cake display being set up each morning. Fresh bakes coming out, being placed in the cabinet. Victoria sponge, coffee and hazelnut cake, strawberry shortcake bar. End with a wide shot of the full display. Ambient sound or trending audio.

Why it works: Bakery and cake display content is huge on social. People love watching displays being built. It creates FOMO and a sense of urgency ("get there early before it sells out"). This format is easy to film and endlessly repeatable.
Idea 05
"Jesmond's best kept secret is a cafe on Clayton Road"
Discovery Local Storytelling

Start outside on Clayton Road. Text on screen: "There is a cafe in Jesmond that most people do not know about yet." Walk in. Show the space, the drinks, the food, the vibe. Build it like a mini documentary. End with the location tag and a "you need to try this" caption.

Why it works: "Hidden gem" and "best kept secret" content performs incredibly well because it makes people feel like they are discovering something exclusive. It drives curiosity and saves. Local audiences share this kind of content heavily.
Digital Enhancements
Beyond Social Media

These are quick wins that would make a real difference alongside the social media work.

Google Business Profile (Priority)

This is the single most important quick win. Setting up a Google Business Profile means you appear in Google Maps, "near me" searches, and the local map pack. For a cafe in Jesmond, this is where a huge amount of footfall comes from. People searching "coffee near me" or "cafe Jesmond" on their phone while walking around will find you.

Once live, encourage customers to leave Google reviews. With your product quality, you would build a strong rating quickly. This is free and takes about 20 minutes to set up.

Every day without a Google Business Profile, you are invisible to anyone searching for a cafe in Jesmond on Google Maps. This should be your number one priority.

Website Menu Page

Your website currently does not display your menu. Adding a dedicated menu page with your full food and drinks offering, with prices, would be one of the most visited pages on the site. People check the menu before they visit. If they cannot find it, some of them will just pick somewhere else.

You already have a great menu on Uber Eats and Just Eat. Putting the same information on your own website means you control the presentation and do not lose customers to competitor suggestions on those platforms.

TripAdvisor Listing

Getting Lilybrew listed on TripAdvisor is free and straightforward. Jesmond attracts visitors, parents of students, and tourists exploring Newcastle. TripAdvisor is still one of the top resources these people use to find somewhere to eat or grab coffee.

  • Claim your free listing on TripAdvisor for Business
  • Upload your best photos of the interior, coffee, and food
  • Encourage happy customers to leave a review after their visit

SEO and Local Search

Your website has minimal meta descriptions and no structured data markup. Adding proper SEO basics would help you rank for local search terms that people are actively searching for in Newcastle.

  • LocalBusiness schema markup so Google understands your hours, location, and type
  • Optimised meta titles and descriptions for key pages
  • Alt text on images so they appear in Google Image searches
  • A blog or news section for seasonal content that drives organic traffic
