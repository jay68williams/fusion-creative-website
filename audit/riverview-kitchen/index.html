TripAdvisor Ranking Could Be Much Higher

You are currently #43 of 291 restaurants in Durham on TripAdvisor. With a 4.5 rating and 385 reviews, that is solid. But with the quality of experience you deliver, there is real potential to push into the top 20. The difference between #43 and #15 is significant in terms of visibility, especially for tourists visiting Durham who use TripAdvisor as their primary discovery tool.

TripAdvisor weights review recency heavily. A steady flow of new reviews, even just a handful per week, would push your ranking up significantly. A simple prompt at the end of each visit, a QR code on the bill, or a follow-up on social media encouraging recent visitors to share their experience would make a real difference over the next few months.

Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Today

These are formats that are working right now for cafes and brunch spots on TikTok and Instagram. All of them play to Riverview Kitchen's strengths: incredible food, a beautiful riverside location, and a warm, welcoming vibe.

Idea 01
"POV: Brunch with a riverside view in Durham"
Atmosphere Location Lifestyle

Start with the walk in. Show the river through the window. Pan across a fully loaded brunch table. Pancake stacks, coffee, orange juice, the lot. Let the location do the talking. Use a warm, relaxing audio track. End with a wide shot of the riverside setting.

Why it works: "POV" and "hidden gem" content is one of the highest-performing cafe formats on TikTok. The riverside location gives you a visual hook that 99% of cafes do not have. This kind of video drives saves, shares, and "where is this?" comments, which is exactly how people discover new brunch spots.
Idea 02
"Building the ultimate pancake stack"
Food Close-Up Process Satisfying

Overhead shot. Start with a bare plate. Layer by layer, build the pancake stack: pancakes, fruit, sauce drizzle, toppings, the final dusting. Each layer a quick cut. Satisfying, fast-paced, trending audio. End with someone cutting into it and the whole thing oozing.

Why it works: Pancake stack videos are some of the most shared food content on social media. They are inherently satisfying to watch and they make people hungry. With your signature stacks already being one of the most photographed items in the cafe, this video practically makes itself.
Idea 03
"Bring your dog to brunch at Riverview Kitchen"
Dog-Friendly Lifestyle Community

A montage of dogs at the cafe. Dogs under tables, dogs by the river, dogs eyeing up their owner's brunch. Mix in shots of the food and the setting. Use a fun, upbeat audio track. End with a dog looking directly at the camera. Caption: "We do not judge if you only came for the dog menu."

Why it works: Dog content is universally loved and gets shared widely. Combining it with your dog-friendly cafe and riverside location creates a video that appeals to dog owners, brunch lovers, and Durham locals all at once. Dog-friendly content consistently outperforms standard food content in engagement and shares.
Idea 04
"Saturday morning at Riverview Kitchen"
Behind the Scenes Busy Service Social Proof

Start with the cafe empty at opening. The team setting up. First customers arriving. Then build the energy: kitchen in full swing, orders flying out, every table full, the river sparkling outside. Capture the buzz of a packed Saturday brunch. Use a track that builds in energy to match.

Why it works: Busy service footage is social proof in video form. When people see a packed cafe, they want to be part of it. This format drives bookings because it creates urgency and FOMO. It also shows the human side of the business, the team working hard, the energy of a great morning.
Idea 05
"The view from table 7 this morning"
Location Seasonal Quick Hit

A simple, short video. Camera sits on the table. Pan from the food to the river view. That is it. No voiceover. Just a beautiful shot of breakfast by the river. Do this across different seasons: autumn leaves, frosty mornings, summer sunshine, spring blossom. Build a recurring series.

Why it works: This is low-effort, high-impact content that you could film every single day. It showcases the one thing no competitor can replicate: your location. As a series, it builds anticipation and gives followers a reason to keep coming back. Seasonal variations keep it fresh and evergreen.
