Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film Today

These are formats that are working right now for restaurants on TikTok and Instagram. Every single one plays to Umai Mi's strengths as a pan-Asian restaurant with incredible visual food.

Idea 01
"The sizzle that stops the scroll"
ASMR Food Porn Viral Format

Close-up of a sizzling hotplate being brought to the table. The camera captures the steam rising, the sauce bubbling, the food dancing on the hot surface. Real audio, no music. Let the sizzle do the work. End with the customer's reaction as it arrives. Pure sensory content.

Why it works: ASMR food content is one of the highest-performing categories on TikTok and Instagram. Sizzling hotplates are the ultimate version of this. The sound alone makes people stop scrolling, and the visual of food actively cooking at the table creates an experience viewers want to have themselves. This is shareable, saveable, and craveable all at once.
Idea 02
"The bento box lunch deal you need to know about"
Value Hook Lunch Discovery

Open with the price. Then reveal what you get: the bento box being assembled, compartment by compartment. Each item getting its own close-up moment. The lid goes on. It comes to the table. The lid comes off. The full reveal. "All of this, for that price. Eldon Square. Lunch sorted."

Why it works: Value reveal content consistently outperforms everything else for restaurants. People love to see how much food they get for their money. Bento boxes are visually perfect for this format because the compartments create a natural "reveal" structure. This drives saves, shares, and direct visits from office workers looking for a great lunch nearby.
Idea 03
"How our sushi rolls are made"
Process Behind the Scenes Craft

The full sushi roll process from start to finish. Rice being spread on the nori. Fillings being placed with precision. The roll being formed. The knife cutting through in one clean motion. The pieces being plated beautifully. Satisfying, precise, and mesmerising. Add a trending audio track or keep it natural with just the sounds of the kitchen.

Why it works: Sushi-making content is endlessly watchable. The precision and skill involved makes it genuinely satisfying to watch. It positions Umai Mi as a place of craft and quality, not just another restaurant. Process content also has extremely high completion rates, which the algorithm rewards with wider distribution.
Idea 04
"What does 15 pounds get you at lunch?"
Value Challenge Local

Walk into Umai Mi. Sit down. Order with a budget of fifteen pounds. Show every dish that arrives. Close-ups of each plate. React to the quality and quantity. End with everything laid out on the table. "Fifteen pounds. In Eldon Square. This is Umai Mi."

Why it works: Budget challenge content is one of the most viral restaurant formats on social media. People love seeing how far their money can stretch. It drives comments ("no way, I need to try this"), saves, and tags. For a city centre restaurant, this is the perfect format to capture the lunchtime crowd who are deciding where to eat every single day.
Idea 05
"Behind the pass: Saturday night at Umai Mi"
Kitchen Energy Social Proof

The camera follows the kitchen during a busy Saturday service. Orders being called. Woks flaming. Plates being assembled at speed. The pass filling up with food. Servers collecting orders and heading to the dining room. Quick cuts, high energy, a pumping soundtrack. End with a wide shot of the packed restaurant.

Why it works: Behind-the-scenes kitchen content builds trust, excitement, and social proof all at once. Viewers see the skill, the volume, and the energy. It makes them feel like they are part of something exciting. A packed restaurant on a Saturday night is the best advertisement you will ever create. It tells people: "This is where everyone is eating. You should be here too."
Digital Enhancements
Beyond Social Media

These are strategic changes that would complement the social media work and drive measurable results.

Google Rating Improvement

Your TripAdvisor is 4.5 and OpenTable is 4.4, but Google is at 4.1. Since Google is the most common place people check before choosing a restaurant, closing that gap should be a priority.

  • Respond to every Google review, positive and negative, with genuine and professional replies
  • Encourage happy dine-in customers to leave a Google review with table cards or receipt QR codes
  • Upload professional food photography to your Google Business profile regularly
  • Post weekly updates about specials, events, or new dishes to keep the profile active

Quick win: Even moving from 4.1 to 4.3 on Google could noticeably increase walk-in traffic from people searching "restaurants near me" or "Asian food Newcastle." It is one of the highest-ROI activities you can do.

Instagram Growth Strategy

Going from ~3,000 to 10,000+ followers is very achievable for a restaurant with Umai Mi's food quality and visual appeal. Consistent Reels posting (4-5 per week), featuring the most visually stunning dishes, combined with local hashtags and engagement tactics, would drive rapid growth. Your food is already being photographed and shared by customers. Owning that narrative with your own high-quality content is the next step.

Delivery Platform Optimisation

You are already on Deliveroo, Uber Eats, and Hungry Panda. The opportunity is to drive more traffic to those listings through social media content that specifically promotes delivery. "Did you know you can get Umai Mi delivered?" content, combined with exclusive delivery offers or new menu items, would increase order volume without any additional infrastructure investment.
