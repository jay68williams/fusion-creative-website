Where The Opportunity Is
Five Things We Would Focus On

You already have the reputation, the team and the services. The job is to turn all of that into reach, so the people in Consett and County Durham who have not found you yet see exactly why their next booking should be with Indulgence. These are the five areas we would focus on.

1

Lead with Transformation Content

Before-and-after videos are the single most powerful format in the beauty industry on social media. Lash sets, brow transformations, tanning results, hair extensions, body contouring. The reveal moment is what stops the scroll, and you are doing these treatments every single day. Film the process, show the result, let the transformation speak for itself.

This is the content that travels beyond your existing followers and reaches people who did not know you existed.

2

Shift from Photos to Short-Form Video

Instagram and TikTok reward video massively over static images. A photo post might reach your current followers. A Reel or TikTok, done properly with a strong hook and trending audio, can reach tens of thousands of people in your area who have never heard of you. The effort of posting is the same, the reach is completely different.

You do not need expensive equipment. A phone, good lighting (which you already have in the salon), and the right editing is enough to start.

3

Showcase the Team and Their Personalities

People book people, not businesses. With seven team members, you have seven different personalities, specialities and stories. "Meet Sam, she has been doing lashes for five years." "Watch Katie do this brow transformation." Individual team content builds familiarity and trust, and it gives clients a reason to book with a specific person.

It also helps with recruitment. When people can see the team, the culture and the atmosphere, the right candidates come to you.

4

Own the Local Search

When someone in Consett or County Durham searches for beauty treatments, tanning, or lashes near them, you need to be the first thing they find. Your website is currently down, which means you are invisible to anyone searching. A simple, well-built site with clear services, booking, and strong local SEO would capture the people actively looking for what you offer.

Combined with a properly maintained Google Business profile and a steady stream of fresh reviews, this is the foundation that turns social visibility into actual bookings.

5

Run Targeted Local Ads to Fill Quiet Days

A small, tightly targeted ad budget on Instagram and Facebook, aimed at women in the Consett and County Durham area, can put your best transformation content and seasonal offers in front of exactly the right audience. The beauty of paid social for a salon is that the targeting is precise and the return is immediate: one new regular client covers months of ad spend.

Use your strongest before-and-after reels as the ads. They already prove the quality, and they feel natural in the feed rather than like a traditional advert.

Ready-Made Content
4 Video Ideas You Could Film in the Salon This Week

These are formats working right now on TikTok and Instagram Reels, built around what you already do every day. Each one could be filmed on a phone during a normal day in the salon.

Idea 01
"Watch this lash transformation"
Before & AfterTransformationReels

A clean, satisfying before-and-after of a lash set, brow lamination, or hair extension fitting. Start with the "before" close-up, a few seconds of the process with trending audio, then the reveal. Under 20 seconds. The format that stops the scroll every time.

Why it works: Transformation content is the most shared and saved format in the beauty space. It shows the quality of your work without saying a word, and it reaches far beyond your current followers.
Idea 02
"A day at Indulgence"
Behind the scenesTeamAtmosphere

A quick montage of a real day: the salon opening up, the team getting set up, clients arriving, treatments in progress, happy faces leaving. Fast cuts, warm lighting, good music. It shows the vibe without trying to sell anything.

Why it works: It builds familiarity with the space and the team. Someone who has never been sees exactly what to expect, and that removes the hesitation of booking somewhere new.
Idea 03
"3 things to know before your first spray tan"
EducationalTipsTanning

Quick, helpful tips from one of the team. What to wear, how to prep, how long it lasts. Delivered straight to camera, friendly and confident. Position yourselves as the experts, not just the service provider.

Why it works: Educational content gets saved and shared. It builds trust with people who have not booked yet and answers the questions they are already Googling. It positions Indulgence as the go-to authority.
Idea 04
"Meet the team: Nicola"
FounderStoryPersonal

A short, personal intro from you as the founder. How you started in beauty in 2012, why you opened Indulgence in 2017, what the salon means to you. Honest, warm, not a sales pitch. Let people see the person behind the business.

Why it works: People book people. A founder story builds an emotional connection that a service list never will, and it makes Indulgence feel like a person, not just a business name.
Digital Enhancements
Beyond the Main Feed

A few extra wins that would compound the content work and turn more of your local visibility into bookings.

Get the Website Back Up and Working Hard

Your website is currently unreachable, which means anyone searching for beauty treatments in Consett cannot find you through Google. A simple, well-designed site with your services, team, reviews, and online booking would capture the people actively looking for what you offer.

  • Clear service pages with descriptions and easy online booking
  • Optimised for "beauty salon Consett", "lashes Consett", "spray tan County Durham" and similar local searches
  • Mobile-first design (most of your audience will find you on their phone)
  • Fresh reviews and transformation galleries that build trust at a glance

Google Business Profile

When someone nearby searches for beauty or tanning, your Google Business profile is often the first thing they see. Keeping it active and fed with fresh photos, posts and reviews makes the difference between being found and being invisible.

  • Keep the profile fully up to date with services, hours and photos
  • Funnel every happy client into a fresh Google review
  • Post weekly updates and offers directly to the profile
  • Turn your best reviews into shareable social content

Seasonal Campaigns and Offers

Beauty is naturally seasonal: prom, weddings, Christmas parties, summer holidays. Planning content and targeted ads around these moments means you are in front of the right people at the exact moment they are looking.

  • A content calendar tied to seasonal peaks and local events
  • Short-run ad campaigns for prom season, wedding season, Christmas and summer
  • Package deals promoted through Reels and Stories that feel timely and relevant

Why it fits Indulgence: With the range of services you offer, there is a natural hook for every season. A well-timed campaign can fill your diary for weeks.
