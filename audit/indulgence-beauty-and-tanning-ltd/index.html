Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film This Week

These are formats working right now for beauty and salon content on TikTok and Instagram, and every one plays to the transformations you already do daily.

Idea 01
"The body contouring before and after"
TransformationBefore / AfterSaves Machine

The measurement before, the treatment, then the reveal with the numbers or the visible difference. Real client, real result, with permission. A clear before, middle, and after in under 20 seconds.

Why it works: Inch-loss and body transformation content is some of the most saved and shared on the platform. People cannot scroll past a real result, and it sends "how much and where do I book" straight to your inbox.
Idea 02
"Watch this hair transformation"
Hair ExtensionsRevealWow

Before shot, the application sped up, then the big turn-around reveal in the mirror and the client's reaction. Slow motion on the final flick of the hair. Let the transformation and the reaction do the work.

Why it works: Hair transformations are a proven viral format. The bigger the change, the further it travels, and the client's genuine reaction is the emotional hook that makes people share it and tag a friend.
Idea 03
"The glow-up tan reveal"
TanningSatisfyingBefore / After

The application, then the develop-and-reveal glow-up, ideally with a getting-ready or event angle ("tan for the weekend"). Clean, satisfying, colourful, with the right trending audio.

Why it works: Tan glow-ups are quick to film, endlessly repeatable, and tap straight into the "getting ready" content people love. Perfect for driving weekend and event bookings on a schedule.
Idea 04
"The most satisfying treatment at Indulgence"
ASMRProcessRetention

A close-up, satisfying treatment: a facial, a fresh set of nails, lashes, or a wax. Tight shots, the sounds, the steps, the finished result. Minimal talking, maximum satisfying detail.

Why it works: Close-up, satisfying beauty process content is some of the highest-retention video there is, which is exactly what pushes a video into more feeds. It also quietly shows the quality and care of your work.
Idea 05
"A day at Indulgence"
TeamBehind The ScenesBrand

Fast-cut footage of the salon in full flow. The team, the treatments, the clients leaving happy, the energy of the place. The personality of Indulgence and the people behind it in 20 seconds.

Why it works: People book salons they feel they already know. Team and behind-the-scenes content builds that familiarity and trust, and it makes a local salon feel like the friendly, obvious choice.

Quick Wins
Things Worth Doing Either Way

A few changes that would make a difference alongside the content, whether you work with us or not.

Turn Your Posts Into Video

You are already creating the content. The single biggest change is switching from photos to short-form video, especially before and afters. The same transformation as a Reel reaches far more new people than the same transformation as a photo.

Quick win: Film your next body contouring or hair transformation as a short video with the before, the process, and the reveal. Post it as a Reel and watch how differently it performs.

Start a TikTok This Week

There is no active TikTok for Indulgence, and beauty content thrives there. Set up the account, post one transformation, and you have opened a brand new channel for reaching local people who have never heard of you.

Build the Google Reviews

Reviews are one of the biggest deciders for a salon, especially locally. A simple system would grow them quickly:

  • A QR code at the desk linking straight to your Google review page
  • A gentle prompt from the team to happy clients as they check out
  • Target: a steadily climbing review count that makes you the obvious choice in Consett
