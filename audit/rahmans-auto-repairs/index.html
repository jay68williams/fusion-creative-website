Ready-Made Content
5 Video Ideas You Could Film This Week

These are formats working right now for auto and mechanic content on TikTok and Instagram, and every one plays to what you already do every day.

Idea 01
"What was actually wrong with this car"
DiagnosticsStoryHook

Start with the symptom: the noise, the warning light, the problem the customer came in with. Walk through finding the fault, then the fix, then the car running right. A clear before, middle, and after. No fancy talking needed, just the story of the job.

Why it works: A problem being solved is one of the most reliable hooks there is. People stay to find out what was wrong, and "I have had that exact noise" comments drive it into more feeds. This one format could carry the account on its own.
Idea 02
"The before and after nobody expected"
Before / AfterSatisfyingReveal

A worn, filthy, or seized part next to the clean new one. A grimy engine bay brought back to life. A rusted brake disc swapped for fresh. Tight close-ups, a slow reveal, the right audio. Pure satisfying payoff.

Why it works: Before and after content is made to be shared and saved. The bigger the transformation, the more it travels, and it quietly proves the quality of your work to every potential customer who sees it.
Idea 03
"POV: your mobile mechanic turns up"
MobilePOVRelatable

First person from the call-out. Pulling up to the drive, finding the fault on the spot, fixing it there and then, the customer back on the road. Show how easy and convenient it is to have the mechanic come to you.

Why it works: POV and "day in the life" content makes people feel like they are right there with you, and it sells the mobile convenience without a word of selling. It is exactly the angle a standard garage cannot match.
Idea 04
"How a full service is actually done"
ProcessASMRTrust

A brake change, an oil and filter service, a part swap, filmed close and clean. The tools, the sounds, the steps, sped up where it helps. Show people exactly what they are paying for and how it should be done properly.

Why it works: Process and tool content is oddly satisfying and high-retention, which is what pushes a video into more feeds. It also builds trust fast: people choose the mechanic who shows them the work over the one who hides it.
Idea 05
"They thought they needed a new engine"
Money-SaverTrustReaction

A job where another quote was way over the top, or a simple fix saved the customer a fortune. Tell the story honestly, show the real problem, and the happy customer at the end. Lean into being the honest mechanic.

Why it works: "Honest mechanic saves customer money" is a story people love to share, because everyone has been burned somewhere. It builds you a reputation as the trustworthy one in the area, which is exactly what gets you booked.
