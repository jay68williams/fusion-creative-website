Content Concepts

8 videos we would make for Freight

These are starting points, not a fixed list. Half of the best videos we make come out of things that happen on the night.

01

How drunk is this person?

Show punters a photo or clip of someone at the bar, let them guess how drunk they are, then breathalyse the person and reveal the score. Reaction-driven, high replay value.

02

How drunk are you?

Ask people at the bar how drunk they think they are, breathalyse them, and catch their reaction when the number hits. Works every weekend.

03

Summer at Freight

A rolling showcase of what is coming up. Events, DJ sets, themed nights, bank holiday takeovers. Builds anticipation and turns the feed into a "what is on" guide.

04

Tell us X for free entry

Short on-the-door prompts. Name three drinks on our menu, tell us your worst date, sing a line of a song. Instant comedy, instant engagement, gets people sharing with mates before they even arrive.

05

Best pick up lines

Stop the lads at the bar. "What is your best pick up line?" The answers range from gold to tragic. Always pulls views, always gets shared.

06

What is on the menu

Fast, close-up, trending-audio shots of the food and drinks. Plates going down, pints being pulled, cocktails being made. The kind of content that makes people screenshot and send to their group chat.

07

Weirdest drink requests

Bar staff telling the camera the strangest orders they have ever had. Bar staff are underrated characters. Give them a minute of screen time and they carry the whole feed.

08

Whatever is trending

Weekly pickups of whatever format is exploding on TikTok and Reels that week, adapted to Freight. Speed is the edge here. We move on a trend within 48 hours, not two weeks.
