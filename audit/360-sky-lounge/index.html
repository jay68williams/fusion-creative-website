Digital Enhancements
Beyond Social Media

These are strategic changes that would make a real difference alongside the social media work.

Build a Review Generation System

16 TripAdvisor reviews does not reflect the volume of customers coming through the door. A systematic approach would transform your online reputation within months:

  • QR codes on tables linking directly to TripAdvisor and Google review pages
  • Staff trained to prompt happy customers with "If you enjoyed it, a quick review would really help us"
  • Follow-up messages after delivery orders with a direct review link
  • Target: 100+ reviews within 6 months, pushing the rating up and the ranking with it

Quick win: Even getting to 50 TripAdvisor reviews would significantly improve your ranking from #76 in Nightlife. Each positive review pushes the average up and buries any unfair negative ones. The reviews are there waiting to happen. Your customers just need the prompt.

Leverage Uber Eats and Just Eat with Social Proof Content

You are already on both delivery platforms, which is a strong foundation. Now connect them to your social media. Film the desserts being carefully packed. Show a delivery rider picking up an order. Create "what I ordered from 360 Sky Lounge" style content. This drives online ordering while also creating content that performs well on social media.

Delivery content works particularly well because it shows viewers they can enjoy the food without leaving home. It widens your audience beyond people who can physically visit the venue.

Create a Signature Hashtag and UGC Strategy

The venue is inherently Instagram-worthy. The rooftop views, the interiors, the desserts. People are already taking photos when they visit. But without a branded hashtag and an active UGC (user-generated content) strategy, that content is scattered and disconnected.

  • Create a signature hashtag like #360SkyLounge and display it prominently in the venue
  • Encourage visitors to tag the account and use the hashtag for a chance to be featured
  • Repost the best customer content to Stories, building community and social proof
  • Consider a "best photo of the month" incentive to drive participation

Why this matters: UGC is the most trusted form of marketing. When potential customers see real people enjoying the venue, it is more persuasive than any ad. And it is free content. Every visitor becomes a potential content creator for you.
