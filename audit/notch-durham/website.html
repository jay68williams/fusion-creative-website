Our Story

Bold Flavours,
Laid-Back Vibes

Notch is Durham's home for American-style BBQ, handcrafted cocktails, and good times. Perched on Elvet Bridge overlooking the river, we combine bold flavours with a laid-back atmosphere that keeps people coming back.

From slow-smoked brisket to signature cocktails, everything at Notch is crafted with care. Whether you are settling in for a weekend bottomless brunch, sharing a BBQ tray with friends, or enjoying a cocktail on the terrace with riverside views, there is always something to come back for.
