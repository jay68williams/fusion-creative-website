# Fusion Creative Website Development Brief for Gravity

## Project Overview
Complete redesign and rebuild of Fusion Creative's marketing agency website (current: fusioncreative.uk) with premium 3D animations and interactive scroll-based elements.

## Services We Offer
- Website Development
- Social Media Management
- Social Content Creation
- Paid Marketing/Advertising

---

## Design Direction & Visual Style

### Color Palette & Aesthetic
- Light, airy gradient backgrounds (white → soft blue)
- Reference: **mailkit.app** gradient style BUT lighter/brighter (no dark gradients)
- Clean, modern, premium feel
- Spacious layouts with strong visual hierarchy

### Overall Layout Structure
- Use **mailkit.app** layout structure as foundation
- Adapt sections to showcase our four core services
- Maintain their clean spacing and section organization

---

## Hero Section - Interactive 3D Phone Animation

### Technical Stack
**Use Spline for all 3D elements and animations** (already installed in Gravity)

### Animation Concept (Inspired by web.meetcleo.com)
Create an interactive 3D phone model that responds to scroll:

**Scroll Behavior:**
1. **Start**: User sees close-up of phone screen content
2. **As user scrolls down**: Camera smoothly zooms out revealing the full 3D phone device
3. **Similar to**: The Cleo website's scroll-to-reveal phone animation

**Phone Screen Content (animated):**
Show a "viral moment" happening in real-time. Choose one approach:
- **Option A**: iMessage/SMS notifications popping up saying "Your post went viral! 🔥"
- **Option B**: Instagram analytics dashboard with engagement numbers rapidly climbing
- **Option C**: Social media feed showing viral content with exploding metrics

Reference the style/quality of **Spectrum's** animations for polish.

### Purpose
The hero immediately *demonstrates* what we deliver (viral content, engagement, results) rather than just describing it.
