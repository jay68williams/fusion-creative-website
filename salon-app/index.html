/ The Problem

The booking apps take a cut of every new client.

Treatwell charges up to 35% of a new client's first booking, 42% once VAT is added. Booksy and Fresha stack monthly subscriptions, card fees and their own cut of new clients on top. Every one of them lists you right next to the salon down the road, then keeps the relationship.

Then there are the no-shows. A last-minute cancel on a colour is a full chair gone, and there is nothing you can do to fill it.

/ Questions
Do I have to leave Treatwell, Fresha or Booksy?

No. Plenty of salons run both: keep a platform for reach if you want, and push your regulars to book direct on your own app where you keep everything. Every client who moves across is money back in your pocket and a relationship you own.

What is actually included for £99 a month?

The whole platform: your branded app, 24/7 booking, deposits, payments, loyalty, push notifications and reminders, plus hosting, support and changes. No commission on top, ever.

Is it a real app on the Apple App Store and Google Play?

Yes. We publish your app to both stores under your own name, so clients can search for you, download it, and keep it on their phone like any other app, with your icon on their home screen. We handle the whole submission and approval process for you.

How do the deposits work?

You choose a deposit amount, and clients pay it by card when they book. It comes off their bill in the salon, and if they no-show or cancel late, you keep it. It is the single fastest way to cut empty chairs.

How does the loyalty card work?

Every client gets your loyalty card inside the app, and they can add it to Apple Wallet so it lives on their phone like a bank card. They earn points on every visit, unlock rewards, and get push offers that bring them back, so there is a real reason to book with you direct.

Will my clients really use their own salon's app?

Yes, when it is genuinely good. The chains push their own apps hard precisely because direct clients rebook more often and cost far less to keep. The key is a fast, clean app that is easy to book from, which is the part we handle.

Is there a contract?

Yes. The plan runs on a 12-month minimum term at £99 a month, then continues monthly. That covers the build, the app stores, hosting, support and updates, and it still costs less over the year than most salons hand the booking platforms and lose to no-shows.
