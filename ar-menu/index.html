How it works

Three steps. We handle everything.

01

We photograph your dishes

We come to you and photograph each dish from multiple angles. No special setup needed. We work around your normal service.

02

We build the 3D models

Using AI photogrammetry, we turn each dish into a detailed 3D model optimised for mobile AR. Every texture, every garnish, every detail.

03

Your AR menu goes live

We deploy a branded AR menu page with your dishes and give you a QR code. Stick it on your tables, printed menus, or window. Customers scan and see your food in AR.

Why AR menus

Give customers a reason to order more

01

No app download

Works straight from the camera on iPhone and Android. Customers scan a QR code and they're in. Zero friction.

02

Increase average order value

When people can see the dish before ordering, they order more confidently. Upsells on premium plates happen naturally when they look this good.

03

Built-in social content

Customers share AR experiences on their stories and feeds. Your restaurant gets free organic exposure from every table.

04

Stand out from every competitor

Nobody else in your area is doing this. It's a genuine talking point. Press, bloggers, and food influencers notice restaurants doing things differently.

05

Break the language barrier

Tourists and international guests don't need to read the menu. They see the dish. Works especially well in city centres with high tourist footfall.

06

Faster table turns

Customers decide quicker when they can see what they're getting. Less deliberation, fewer questions to staff, faster orders.
